## 🇺🇸 English (U.S.) — Primary

### Description
The basketball strategy board built for real timeouts.

No clutter. No accounts. No loading screens. Just open the app and start coaching.

PLAY ANIMATION & VIDEO EXPORT:
Record your plays in real time — move players, draw arrows, and capture every step. Export as MP4 video and share with your team via AirDrop, Messages, LINE, or any app. Your players see the play in motion, not just arrows on a static diagram.

FULL DRAWING TOOLKIT:
- 5 line types: Cut, Pass, Dribble, Screen, and freehand
- 5 colors for clear, layered diagrams
- Undo and erase with one tap

REALISTIC COURT:
- Half court and full court modes (FIBA regulation)
- Drag players and balls freely
- Ball snaps to player hands automatically
- Vision cones to visualize player field of view
- Toggle arms display on/off
- Triple-tap any player to edit jersey number or remove

DESIGNED FOR COACHES:
No subscriptions. No cloud sync. No complex menus. Basketball Playboard works exactly like the whiteboard you already use — but you can record it, export it, and share it.

Save and load unlimited plays. Build your full playbook on one device.

WORKS EVERYWHERE:
- Portrait and landscape orientation
- iPhone and iPad optimized
- Apple Pencil supported
- 100% offline — no internet required

PRIVACY:
Zero data collection. No analytics. No tracking. Everything stays on your device.

## ASO Notes (internal, not submitted)

### Keyword Strategy
- EN: Covers core terms (basketball, coach, strategy, whiteboard, playbook) + secondary (tactics, drill, clipboard, court, animation). "playbook" and "clipboard" are high-search terms competitors miss.
- JP: 「バスケ」is the #1 casual search term (not バスケットボール alone). 「作戦ボード」is the exact phrase coaches search. Both included.

### Title+Subtitle Optimization
- App Name uses the product name for brand recognition
- Subtitle packs in the most searched terms not in the title
- EN subtitle: "Basketball Coaching Whiteboard" — hits "coaching" and "whiteboard"
- JP subtitle: 「バスケ作戦ボード・動画で共有」— hits 「バスケ」「作戦ボード」「動画」「共有」

### Description Optimization
- First 3 lines are critical (visible before "more" tap) — hook + value prop
- Feature list uses searchable terms naturally
- Japanese version uses natural phrasing, not direct translation
- Key differentiators emphasized: video export, no subscription, offline

### Secondary Category
- "Education" added — coaches searching in Education category can also find the app

### Competitor Keywords NOT Used (already in title/subtitle)
- "basketball" is in app name, so excluded from keywords where possible to save space
- Focus keywords on terms NOT in title/subtitle
