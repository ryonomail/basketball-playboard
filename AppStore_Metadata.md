## Description
The basketball strategy board built for real timeouts.

No clutter. No accounts. No loading screens. Just open the app and start coaching.

FEATURES:
- Drag players and balls on a realistic court
- Half court and full court modes
- Record play animations with real-time motion capture
- Export plays as MP4 videos to share with your team
- 5 drawing tools: Cut, Pass, Dribble, Screen, and freehand lines
- 5 line colors for clear diagrams
- Vision cones to visualize player awareness
- Triple-tap to edit player numbers or remove players
- Add multiple balls for practice drills
- Ball snaps to player hands automatically
- Works in portrait and landscape
- All data stored locally on your device
- No internet required. 100% offline.

DESIGNED FOR COACHES:
Basketball Playboard eliminates every unnecessary feature found in other coaching apps. No complex menus, no subscription fees, no cloud sync headaches. It works exactly like the whiteboard you already use — but better.

Record your set plays, practice drills, and timeout strategies. Export them as videos to share via AirDrop, Messages, or any app. Your players see the play in motion, not just arrows on a static diagram.

PRIVACY:
Zero data collection. No analytics. No tracking. Everything stays on your device.
