Basketball Playboard Support

Getting Started

Basketball Playboard is a simple, powerful strategy board for basketball coaches. Drag players and balls on the court, draw plays, record animations, and export videos.

FAQ

How do I move players?

Tap and drag from the center of a player to move them. Drag from the edge to rotate their facing direction.

How do I change a player's number?

Triple-tap on a player to edit their number or delete them.

How do I record a play?

Tap the red record button, move players and balls to animate the play, then tap stop. Give it a name and save.

How do I export a video?

Load a saved play, then tap the share button in the playback bar. The play will be rendered as an MP4 video.

How do I add or remove balls?

Tap the orange + button to add a ball. Triple-tap a ball to remove it (minimum 1 ball).

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
