Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

Basketball Playboard ("the App") is a basketball strategy board application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data (saved plays, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

No Network Access

The App does not require or use an internet connection. All functionality works entirely offline.

Data Storage

Saved plays are stored in your device's local storage (Documents directory). This data is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the App will remove all stored data.

Video Export

When you export a play as a video, the file is created locally on your device. Sharing is initiated by you through the iOS share sheet and is handled by the operating system.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page.
